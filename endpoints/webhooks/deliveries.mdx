<Info>
  **GET** `/webhooks/{webhook_id}/deliveries` · Scope: `webhooks:manage`
</Info>

Every delivery attempt — `delivered`, `failed`, or `pending` — with `http_status`, `attempts`, and the first 2,000 chars of your endpoint's response.

Cross-reference `delivery_id` with the `X-Bareconnect-Delivery` header in your own logs to correlate webhook deliveries with your application traces.
